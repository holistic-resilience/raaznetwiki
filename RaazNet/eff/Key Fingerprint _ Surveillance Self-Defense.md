# Key Fingerprint

A sequence of letters or numbers that represent a public [key ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/key). Some privacy tools let you check the match between someone's [key fingerprint ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/key-fingerprint) as seen by your device and by their device. The purpose of this check is to prevent a [man-in-the-middle attack ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/man-in-the-middle-attack), where someone tricks you into using the wrong key.
