# Internet filtering

Filtering is a polite term for [blocking ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/internet-filtering) or censoring internet traffic. Virtual Private Networks or services like Tor are sometimes used to access internet communications that would otherwise be filtered.
