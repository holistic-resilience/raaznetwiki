# Passphrase

A [passphrase ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/passphrase) is a kind of [password ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/password). We use "passphrase" to convey the idea that a password which is a single word is far too short to protect you and a longer phrase is much better. The webcomic XKCD has a good explanation. [http://xkcd.com/936/](http://xkcd.com/936/)
