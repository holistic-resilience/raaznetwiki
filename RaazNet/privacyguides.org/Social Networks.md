# Social Networks

[Edit this page](https://github.com/privacyguides/privacyguides.org/blob/main/docs/social-networks.md?plain=1 "Edit this page")

Protects against the following threat(s):

- [Censorship](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/basics/common-threats/#avoiding-censorship)
- [Surveillance Capitalism](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/basics/common-threats/#surveillance-as-a-business-model)

These privacy-respecting **social networks** allow you to participate in online communities without giving up excessive personal information.

## Mastodon

**Mastodon** is a decentralized social network based on ActivityPub.

## Element

**Element** is the flagship client for the Matrix protocol.

## Criteria

**Please note we are not affiliated with any of the projects we recommend.**

- Must be free and open-source.
- Must use a federated protocol.
- Must make public content accessible without an account.
