# Encryption key

A piece of information that is used to convert a message into an unreadable form. In some cases, you need the same [encryption key ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/encryption-key) to decode the message. In others, the encryption key and decryption [key ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/key) are different.
