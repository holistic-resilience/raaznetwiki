# Interested in encryption?

[Encryption ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/encryption) is one of the most powerful tools we have to protect our [data ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/data) and our communication, but it can be overwhelming to learn about. These guides start out with the basics and move all the way through the deeper concepts of [public key encryption ![](https://ssd.eff.org/assets/efforg/info-7ae1fb29e1e734b20023f3e3470316c82b84a7e4b9f1d1e2f49e399f1f8de2d3.png)](https://ssd.eff.org/glossary/public-key-encryption).
