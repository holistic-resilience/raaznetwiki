# Digital Security Guides & Resources

Strong digital security practices enable more secure reporting. These guides and resources empower you with the knowledge and tools needed to stay ahead in the ever-evolving landscape of digital security.

**Topics covered include:**
- Encrypting text messages
- Locking down social media accounts
- Protecting against hackers
- Mitigating online harassment

Resources are designed for beginners to experts.
