# Digital Security Guides & Resources

Strong digital security practices enable more secure reporting.

**Protect yourself and your sources** with expert advice on digital security from Freedom of the Press Foundation's training team.

These guides and resources empower journalists with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate the evolving landscape of digital security. Topics range from encrypting text messages and securing social media accounts to protecting against hackers and mitigating online harassment—suitable for beginners through experts.
