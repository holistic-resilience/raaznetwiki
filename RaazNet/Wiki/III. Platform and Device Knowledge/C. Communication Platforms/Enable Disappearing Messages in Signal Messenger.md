# How to Enable Disappearing Messages in Signal Messenger

Disappearing Messages is one of the practical and important features in Signal messenger. This feature allows sent messages to be automatically deleted from both sides of the conversation after a specified period of time.

## Why use this feature?

- **Increased privacy**: Messages are automatically deleted after being read
- **Reduced digital footprint**: Sensitive information is not stored permanently
- **Greater security**: In case of unauthorized access to the device, old messages are not visible
