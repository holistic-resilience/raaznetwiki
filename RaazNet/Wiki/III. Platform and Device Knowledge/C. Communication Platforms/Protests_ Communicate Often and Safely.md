# Protests: Communicate Often and Safely

For a successful protest, establish a clear communication plan for monitoring developments, coordinating actions, and responding to emergencies. Being prepared for unexpected situations means knowing who to contact, when, and how.

## Establish an External Contact

You or a member of your affinity group should maintain regular communication with a trusted contact outside the protest who can raise the alarm if something happens.

**With your external contact, agree on:**
- Scheduled check-in times
- Security indicators (code words or phrases)
- Actions to take if something goes wrong

Consider coordinating through civil society organisations and human rights groups when necessary.

## Prepare Emergency Contact Information

Create a list of people and organisations you can contact in emergencies, including a lawyer.

**Storage recommendations:**
- If storing on your mobile, consider using codenames
- Carry a written backup copy in case you lose your phone, decide not to bring it, or run out of battery
- Write the most critical numbers on your arms or legs in case possessions are lost or confiscated

## Understand Surveillance Risks

Mobile phone calls, SMS messages, and many common messenger applications are easy to eavesdrop on. Authorities and other adversaries increasingly conduct surveillance of communication channels during protests.

**Key consideration:** Simply having your mobile device at a protest can identify you and potentially be used against you.

## Use Encrypted Communication Tools

If you decide to use mobile phones, choose end-to-end encrypted applications.

### During the Protest
| Purpose | Recommended Tool | Platform |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| Encrypted messaging and calls | [Signal](https://signal.org/) | Android, iPhone |
| Encrypted SMS | [Silence](https://silence.im/) | Android |

### Before and After the Protest
| Purpose | Recommended Tool |
|---------|------------------|
| Encrypted email | [GPG with Thunderbird](https://securityinabox.org/en/guide/thunderbird/windows) |
| Encrypted voice/video calls | [Jitsi Meet](https://meet.jit.si/) |
| Encrypted chat (computer) | [Jitsi](https://jitsi.org/) |
| Encrypted chat (mobile) | [ChatSecure](https://chatsecure.org/) |
