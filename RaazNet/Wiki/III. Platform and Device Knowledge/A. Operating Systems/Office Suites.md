# Office Suites

Choose an **office suite** that does not require logging in to an account to access its full functionality. The tools listed here can be used offline and could reasonably act as a replacement for Microsoft Office for most needs.

## LibreOffice

**LibreOffice** is a free and open-source office suite with extensive functionality.

- **Homepage**: [libreoffice.org](https://libreoffice.org/)
- **Privacy Policy**: [View Policy](https://libreoffice.org/about-us/privacy/privacy-policy-en)
- **Documentation**: [English Documentation](https://documentation.libreoffice.org/en/english-documentation)
- **Source Code**: [View Source](https://libreoffice.org/about-us/source-code)

## Selection Criteria

Privacy Guides is not affiliated with any recommended projects. Their recommendations are based on the following requirements:

- Must be **cross-platform**
- Must be **open-source software**
- Must **function offline**
- Must support editing **documents, spreadsheets, and slideshows**
- Must **export files to standard document formats**

For more details on evaluation methodology, see the [Privacy Guides standard criteria](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/about/criteria/).
