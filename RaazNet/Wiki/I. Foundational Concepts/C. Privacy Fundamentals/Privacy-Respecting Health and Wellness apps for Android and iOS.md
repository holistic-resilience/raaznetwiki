# Privacy-Respecting Health and Wellness Apps

Keep track of your health and fitness-related goals with these apps. Unlike their mainstream alternatives, your personal health information will be kept private.

## Why Privacy Matters for Health Apps

Popular health trackers are notorious for collecting and sharing user data. Depending on your jurisdiction, this may lead to legal consequences or privacy violations affecting your personal autonomy.

---

## Menstrual Cycle Tracking

### Drip

**Platform:** Android, iOS

**Drip** is a gender-inclusive and open-source menstrual cycle tracker. It relies on the "sympto-thermal method" to predict ovulation.

**Privacy Features:**
- All user data stored locally on device
- Password protection available
- Open-source code for transparency

**Links:**
- [Homepage](https://bloodyhealth.gitlab.io/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://bloodyhealth.gitlab.io/privacy-policy.html)
- [Source Code](https://gitlab.com/bloodyhealth/drip)
- Available on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.drip) and [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/drip/id1584564949)

### Euki

**Platform:** Android, iOS

**Euki** is a nonprofit-backed menstrual cycle tracker that also serves as a medication tracker and sexual wellness knowledge base.

**Privacy Features:**
- Automatic deletion scheduling for personal data
- All user data stored locally on device
- Password protection available

**Links:**
- [Homepage](https://eukiapp.org/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://eukiapp.org/privacy-policy)
- [Source Code](https://github.com/Euki-Inc/Euki-Android)
- Available on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kollectivemobile.euki) and [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/euki/id1469213846)

## Fitness Trackers

General-purpose apps for counting steps, tracking sleep, and measuring heartbeat.

### Apple Fitness

**Platform:** iOS

**Apple Fitness** is the default fitness app for iOS.

**Privacy Features:**
- End-to-end encryption when syncing across devices
- Most data processed on-device

**Links:**
- [Privacy Policy](https://apple.com/legal/privacy/consumer-health-personal-data/en-ww)
- Available on [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1208224953)

### Gadgetbridge

**Platform:** Android

**Gadgetbridge** is an open-source application that allows you to pair and manage Bluetooth fitness devices without relying on vendor applications.

**Privacy Features:**
- No third-party data collection
- Works with compatible smartwatches independently
- Fully open-source

> **Note:** Review the [compatibility list](https://gadgetbridge.org/gadgets) before purchasing a device. Some devices require initial vendor app connection before Gadgetbridge can be used.

**Links:**
- [Homepage](https://gadgetbridge.org/)
- [Documentation](https://gadgetbridge.org/basics)
- [Source Code](https://codeberg.org/Freeyourgadget/Gadgetbridge)
- Available on [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/nodomain.freeyourgadget.gadgetbridge)

## Health Records

Apps for collecting, managing, and sharing personal health data with providers.

### Apple Health Records

**Platform:** iOS

**Apple Health Records** is a built-in feature within Apple Health for viewing, storing, and sharing health records.

**Privacy Features:**
- End-to-end encryption
- On-device processing
- Shares security features with Apple Fitness

**Links:**
- [Homepage](https://apple.com/health)
- [Privacy Policy](https://apple.com/legal/privacy/consumer-health-personal-data/en-ww)

### CommonHealth

**Platform:** Android (United States only)

**CommonHealth** allows access to electronic health records with secure sharing to providers.

**Privacy Features:**
- All health data stored on device
- Passcode or biometric authentication protection
- [Developer SDK is open source](https://github.com/the-commons-project)

> **Note:** The app itself is closed source, though the developer SDK is open source.

**Links:**
- [Homepage](https://commonhealth.org/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://commonhealth.org/privacy)
- Available on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.thecommonsproject.android.phr)

## Selection Criteria

All recommended apps meet these minimum requirements:

- **Automatic updates** supported
- **No unencrypted data** stored outside the device
- **Offline functionality** available
