# Privacy Settings in Google and the Privacy Checkup Feature

## Introduction

With the increasing sensitivity of users regarding how their information is used, Google has introduced new security features to increase transparency and return control of information to users. One of these tools is **[Privacy Checkup](https://myaccount.google.com/intro/privacycheckup)**.

> **Important Note:** All items introduced are based on the explanations and claims of Google, and their complete accuracy in practice is not 100% reliable. Before sharing any data on internet services, consider the potential security consequences.

---

## Main Sections of Privacy Checkup

### 1. Web & App Activity History

When this option is active, Google saves the following:

- Search activities on the Google site and related services
- Chrome browser history (searches, visited sites, activities)
- Android apps you use (time and frequency)

**Control Options:**

- Completely disable data collection
- Manual deletion of data
- Auto-delete in 3-month, 6-month, 1-year, or 18-month intervals

---

### 2. Location History

Google records a map of all the places you have been present with your device. This feature is active even when using other Google services.

**Important Note:** Stopping location recording does not delete previous data. To delete previous history, you must go to **Google Maps Timeline**.

---

### 3. Voice & Audio Activity History

If you use **Google Assistant**, your voice commands are recorded and saved. Google uses these sounds to improve the speech recognition system.

**Deactivation:** Proceed from the **Activity Controls** section.

---

### 4. YouTube History

Google saves a history of videos watched and searches on YouTube, including:

- Stop time of watching the video
- Search history
- Suggestions based on your taste

**Note:** YouTube is a social network. Pay attention to the following as well:

- Accounts you follow
- Public playlists
- Visible comments and activities

---

### 5. Ad Settings

This section does not allow disabling advertisements completely, but you can:

- Disable ad personalization
- Change the type of ads displayed

**Recommendation:** To limit advertisements, use Ad Blocker extensions.

---

### 6. Profile Information Control

This section includes information that others see about you:

- Name and date of birth
- Public profile information

**Shared Endorsements:** If active, your reviews may be displayed in Google ads for other users. It is recommended to disable this option.

---

## Final Recommendations

- Check privacy settings **at least twice a year**
- Create a **reminder email** for periodic review of settings
- Check privacy in **all services** you use
