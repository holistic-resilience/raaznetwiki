# Strategies to Counter Smart Scams Using Artificial Intelligence

Today, there are various services based on artificial intelligence that help people create content as legitimate tools. However, security researchers warn that criminals can misuse these tools to commit crimes such as fraud and extortion.

According to recent reports, the use of artificial intelligence by attackers for online scams is increasing significantly.

## Methods of Misusing Artificial Intelligence

Cybercriminals misuse artificial intelligence in the following ways:

### Creating Fake Accounts

Using text and visual content generated by artificial intelligence to create fake user accounts in online scams, social engineering, phishing, and romance scams.

### Impersonation

Using artificial intelligence to impersonate legal authorities and trusted individuals to obtain users' personal information.

### Malicious Websites

Generating content on malicious websites that encourages victims to download malware or disclose personal information.

### Extortion with Fabricated Content

Creating fabricated pornographic images and videos of individuals for extortion.

### Charity Fraud

Producing and publishing fabricated images and videos related to natural disasters to attract financial donations to fake charity organizations.

## Detection and Countermeasure Strategies

### Setting a Family Code

Choose a code word or phrase with family members that is only between yourselves. This helps verify each other's identity in urgent situations.

### Checking for Signs of Fake Images

Look for subtle flaws in images or videos:

- Abnormal shape of hands
- Irregular faces
- Strange shadows
- Unrealistic movements

### Detecting Artificial Voice

Pay attention to unnatural tone or word choice in calls to identify artificial intelligence voice simulation.

### Limiting Public Information

- Avoid excessive publishing of your photos and voice online
- Limit social media privacy settings to trusted friends

### Verifying Calls

After receiving suspicious calls, verify the identity of the person or organization by searching the internet and contacting the official number.

### Protecting Personal Information

- Do not share your important information with strangers
- Avoid sending money, gift cards, or cryptocurrency to unknown persons
