# Protests: Consider What to Bring

What you carry to a protest can either help you respond to emergencies or become a pretext for harassment. Make these decisions only after completing your context and risk analysis, and coordinate with your affinity group to distribute resources according to each person's role.

## Items to Avoid

- **Electronic devices** without first reviewing their contents. Consider whether sensitive information is stored on them. Remember that mobile phones can track your location—this has been used to identify and target peaceful protesters.
- **Anything potentially incriminating**: items that could be considered weapons, illegal drugs, or alcohol.

## Essential Items to Bring

### Health and Safety
- Any special medication you require
- First aid kit
- Toilet paper and sanitary products
- Swimming goggles and bandana or scarf (protection against tear gas)
- Antacid solution for treating chemical burns

### Sustenance
- High-energy food
- Water for drinking and wound cleaning
- Supplies to last longer than the planned protest duration

### Communication and Emergency
- Some cash for emergencies
- Emergency contacts written on your arm or leg
- If bringing a phone: consider a new SIM card and burner phone with pre-loaded credit and spare battery

### Clothing and Documentation
- Comfortable clothing allowing easy movement
- Camera for documenting abuses (optional)
- Identification: coordinate with your affinity group—in some countries, lacking ID invites arrest; in others, carrying it may be used against you

## Preparing for Searches

Authorities commonly stop and search protesters, ostensibly to prevent weapons or illegal items from entering protests. However, searches are also used for intimidation or harassment.

**Before the protest:**
- Consult a lawyer about legal constraints on police searches in your context
- Understand both the official codes of practice and practical realities
- Prepare and practice verbal responses if resistance is legally possible
- Ensure you carry nothing illegal

**During the protest:**
- Alert your affinity group immediately if you're stopped so everyone remains aware
