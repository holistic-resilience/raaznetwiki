# Why IoT Devices Are More at Risk of Cyber Security Than Imagined

As people's connection with smart digital devices (IoT devices) increases, so do the security threats related to them. In this post, some of the risks and threats present in these devices for users and businesses are discussed.

## The Expansion of Smart Device Usage

When the subject of IoT devices comes up, they are generally considered novice products that need to prove themselves. This issue goes back to about 20 years ago, when people were connecting to the Internet for the first time with their desktop computers and laptops.

Today, we are at a relatively similar level in the common consumption of IoT devices. Many people in homes and organizations in office bureaus, **without paying attention to the issue of security**, use these devices:

- Smart displays
- Sensors
- Smart motors
- Other network-connected equipment

## Benefits and Risks

### Benefits of IoT Devices

- Reduction of various costs
- Saving on energy consumption
- Increasing the efficiency of organizations

### Security Risks

If these devices, like other products connected to the Internet, do not possess adequate security, they can pave the way for cyber attackers.

**Main problem:** Many IoT devices are built almost without considering security layers and standards.

## Practical Example: Smart Aquarium

A simple example: A smart water tank of a fish aquarium that is controllable via the Internet.

This case may not be important in terms of security from the view of many users, but:

> When this smart tool connects to the user's internet network, due to the existence of security weaknesses and multiple vulnerabilities, it exposes the security of **all connected devices** to the user's internet on the same network to serious risks.

Imagine the security of your laptop or mobile being compromised solely because of a smart aquarium water tank.

## Security Recommendations

Typically, every few months, news related to vulnerabilities and security flaws in IoT devices is published.

**Before purchasing smart devices:**

- Pay attention to the importance of the security of this equipment
- Check the correct way to use them in your networks
- Ensure the regular updating of the devices' software
