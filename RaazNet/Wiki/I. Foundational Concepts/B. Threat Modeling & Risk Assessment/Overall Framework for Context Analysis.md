# Overall Framework for Context Analysis

Carrying out a systematic analysis of our context underpins our ability to design and maintain accurate security strategies. As we and our environment change, so too does our security situation.

## Why Context Analysis Matters

The threats we face are constantly changing, and our security strategy will only be effective if we acknowledge this and plan accordingly. Taking security precautions out of habit or based on hearsay may leave us vulnerable when circumstances change.

While this manual presents the process once, these tools should be used iteratively in accordance with your own planning processes to remain current and effective.

## Analysis and Perception

The framework outlined here helps us be as objective as possible in diagnosing our security situation. However, security remains fundamentally a question of perception.

Our perception of threats may be challenged, limited, or flawed in two key ways:

| Perception Gap | Description | Example |
|----------------|-------------|---------|
| **Unrecognized Threats** | Dangers we cannot identify due to unfamiliar environments or concealed tactics | Electronic surveillance, sophisticated social engineering |
| **Unfounded Fears** | Things posing no actual danger that we perceive as threats | Misinformation from adversaries, anxiety from past trauma |

![Perception of Threats Diagram](https://holistic-security.tacticaltech.org/ckeditor_assets/pictures/23/content_perceptionthreats.png)

## Improving Perception Accuracy

Getting a realistic assessment of threat levels is challenging but achievable. The goal is reducing both unrecognized threats and unfounded fears so that security practices remain effective.

**Strategies for better accuracy:**

- **Undertake research and analysis** — Gather information systematically rather than relying on assumptions
- **Verify perceptions with trusted contacts** — Check your understanding with colleagues who have relevant experience
- **Review regularly** — Update your analysis as situations evolve
