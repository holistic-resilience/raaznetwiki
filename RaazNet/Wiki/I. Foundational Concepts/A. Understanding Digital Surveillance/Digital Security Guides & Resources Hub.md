# Digital Security Guides & Resources Hub

Strong digital security practices enable more secure reporting. This resource hub from Freedom of the Press Foundation provides guides and tools to help journalists, activists, and privacy-conscious users protect themselves and their sources.

## Overview

These guides and resources empower you with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate the evolving landscape of digital security. Topics range from encrypting text messages and securing social media accounts to protecting against hackers and mitigating online harassment—suitable for beginners to experts.
