# Four Safer Alternatives to the Zoom Platform

After the COVID-19 pandemic, the Zoom platform became the most popular tool for video calls. However, Zoom's announcement regarding the use of user audio and video for artificial intelligence has increased privacy concerns. In this article, four suitable alternatives are introduced.

---

## 1. Jitsi Meet — The Safest Option

**Key Features:**

- Open-source software
- End-to-end encryption
- Screen sharing
- No registration required

**Suitable for:** Users for whom privacy is the main priority and want to avoid web tracking.

---

## 2. Google Meet — Efficient and Widely Used

**Key Features:**

- Integration with Google Calendar and Google Drive
- Automatic meeting link generation
- File sharing in chat

**Free Version:**

- Maximum 100 participants
- One-hour meetings
- Google account required

---

## 3. Microsoft Teams — Comprehensive Collaboration Tool

**Key Features:**

- Integration with Microsoft 365
- Individual and group audio and video calls
- File and screen sharing
- Document collaboration with Office Online

**Free Version:**

- Maximum 300 participants
- 2 GB storage per user
- 10 GB per team

> **⚠️ Note:** Microsoft has integrated artificial intelligence with Teams and retains meeting data including recordings, attendance reports, and names of invitees.

---

## 4. Zoho Meeting — Suitable for Businesses

**Key Features:**

- Easy screen sharing
- Remote system access
- Technical support for camera and microphone issues

**Suitable for:** Companies and organizations that use Zoho services and care about privacy.

---

| Service         | End-to-end Encryption | Open Source | Max Participants (Free) | Privacy Concern |
| --------------- | --------------------- | ----------- | ----------------------- | --------------- |
| Jitsi Meet      | ✅                    | ✅          | Unlimited               | Low             |

---

## Conclusion

For maximum privacy, **Jitsi Meet** is the best choice. For integration with existing tools, **Google Meet** or **Microsoft Teams** are suitable, but privacy concerns must be considered.
