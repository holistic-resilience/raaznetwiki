# Protests: Communicate Often and Safely

For a successful protest, establish a clear communication plan for monitoring developments, coordinating actions, and responding to emergencies. Being prepared for unexpected situations means knowing who to contact, when, and how.

## Establish an External Contact

You or a member of your affinity group should maintain regular communication with a trusted contact outside the protest who can raise the alarm if something happens.

**With your external contact:**
- Organize scheduled check-in times
- Share security indicators (code words or phrases)
- Agree on actions to take if something goes wrong
- Consider routing communication through civil society organizations or human rights groups if necessary

## Prepare Emergency Contact Information

Create a list of people and organizations you can contact in emergencies, including a lawyer.

**Storage recommendations:**
- If storing on your mobile, consider using codenames
- Carry a written backup copy in case you lose your phone, leave it behind, or run out of battery
- Consider writing the most critical numbers on your arms or legs in case possessions are lost or confiscated

## Understand Surveillance Risks

Mobile phone calls, SMS messages, and many common messenger applications are easy to eavesdrop on. Communication channels are increasingly monitored during protests by authorities or other adversaries.

**Key considerations:**
- Simply having your mobile device at a protest can identify you
- Location data and metadata can be used against you
- Unencrypted communications may be intercepted

## Use Encrypted Communication Tools

If you decide to use mobile phones for communication, choose end-to-end encrypted options.

### During Protests
| Tool | Platform | Use Case |
|------|----------|----------|
| [Signal](https://signal.org/) | Android, iOS | Encrypted messaging and calls |
| [Silence](https://silence.im/) | Android | Encrypted SMS |

### Before and After Protests
| Tool | Platform | Use Case |
|------|----------|----------|
| GPG/PGP | Computer | Encrypted email |
| [Jitsi Meet](https://meet.jit.si/) | Web, Mobile | Encrypted video/voice calls |
| [Jitsi](https://jitsi.org/) | Computer | Encrypted chat |
| [ChatSecure](https://chatsecure.org/) | Mobile | Encrypted chat |
