## Key Takeaways

1. **Use a password manager** with a strong master passphrase
2. **Enable two-factor authentication** on all important accounts
3. **Be vigilant about phishing** - verify before clicking
4. **Audit browser extensions** regularly and remove unnecessary ones
5. **Have a plan** for responding to data breaches
6. **Consider passkeys** for phishing-resistant authentication
