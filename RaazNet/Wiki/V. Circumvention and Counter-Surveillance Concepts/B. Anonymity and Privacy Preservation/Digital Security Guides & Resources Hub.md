# Digital Security Guides & Resources Hub

Strong digital security practices enable more secure reporting.

**Protect yourself and your sources** with advice on digital security from the Freedom of the Press Foundation. These guides and resources empower you with the knowledge and tools needed to stay ahead in the ever-evolving landscape of digital security.

Topics covered include:
- Encrypting text messages
- Locking down social media accounts
- Protecting against hackers
- Mitigating online harassment

Resources are available for all skill levels, from beginners to experts.
