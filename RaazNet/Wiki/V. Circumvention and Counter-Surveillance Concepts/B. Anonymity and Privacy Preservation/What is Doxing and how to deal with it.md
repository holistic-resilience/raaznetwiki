# What is Doxing and how to deal with it?

## Introduction

Have you ever thought about how dangerous making your social media account public or tagging locations on your photos can be?

Many of us search the names of people we have just met on social media. But the issue is that not everyone does this out of curiosity and may have malicious intentions.

According to a study conducted by the **Victims Commissioner** in the UK, **19 percent** of the statistical population have been targeted by doxing so far.

## What is Doxing?

**Doxing** means collecting and publicly publishing individuals' personal information without their consent. The goal is usually to embarrass, threaten, or harass the victim.

### Features of Doxing

- Can be done by **strangers** or **acquaintances and family members**
- May start from a **small disagreement** on online platforms
- Is common on platforms like Twitter, Twitch, Discord, and Steam

### Serious consequences of Doxing

- Cyberbullying
- Stalking and harassment in the real world
- Physical attacks
- In severe cases, death threats

> **Important Note:** You might say to yourself "I have nothing to hide," but a person who intends to harass you does not care about this issue. We can all be potential victims of this attack.

## Who is most at risk?

### Young generation

- People who spend more time online are more vulnerable
- False sense of security resulting from using a username and avatar
- Minor details can provoke doxers to search further

## Methods to prevent Doxing

There is no definitive way to completely prevent doxing, but you can make the work difficult for doxers:

### Personal information management

- ✅ Do not publish your personal information
- ✅ When publishing images, pay attention to details that could reveal information about you
- ✅ Delete information you have previously published about yourself

### User account security

- ✅ Use **user accounts with different names** on different social networks
- ✅ Enable **two-step verification**
- ✅ Use a **strong and unique password**

### Safe online behavior

- ✅ Refrain from opening links sent by unknown individuals
- ✅ Ensure that video conferences are encrypted

## Necessary actions in case of becoming a victim

### 1. Keep calm

Do not blame yourself for the malicious intentions of others. Remember that we are all at risk.

### 2. Documentation

Take **screenshots** of all details.

### 3. Immediate actions

- **Block and report** the abuser's account
- Temporarily make your accounts **private**

### 4. Notification

Inform your friends and family about this incident.

### 5. Professional help

- If needed, speak with a **counselor**
- If the harassment continues, pursue it through **judicial authorities and the police**
