# Improving Privacy When Using ChatGPT

The use of AI tools and chatbots has increased significantly in recent years. However, the use of user data by the creators of these tools to improve services has raised numerous concerns regarding privacy.

OpenAI has announced that user conversations are shared only with their consent. However, the company still stores conversation history.

If you use ChatGPT and are concerned about privacy, take the following actions:

---

## 1. Using Temporary Chat

![Use the Temporary Chat feature in ChatGPT](https://certfa.com/res/get/i/u/6918d072-1371-4bc1-9f7e-650001e589f0_thumb.jpg)

Temporary chats:

- Are not saved in your activity history
- Are not used to train new models
- Are retained for only 30 days to monitor for potential abuse

---

## 2. Disabling Model Training

![Disable the model improvement feature through training](https://certfa.com/res/get/i/u/66996474-92e5-40c7-ba41-41bc62733a64_thumb.jpg)

Disable the **"Improve the model for everyone"** option.

By doing this:

- Your conversation history will not be used to train ChatGPT
- Your old conversations are also excluded from the training process
