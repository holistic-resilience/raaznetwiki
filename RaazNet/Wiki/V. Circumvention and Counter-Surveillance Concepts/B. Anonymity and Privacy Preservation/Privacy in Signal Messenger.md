# How to Control Privacy More in Signal Messenger

Signal Messenger, as a secure communication tool, has provided various features for controlling the privacy of its users.

## What You Will Learn in This Tutorial

- Disabling **Read Receipts** (Read Receipts / Seen)
- Disabling **Typing Indicators** (Typing Indicators)

## Why Are These Settings Important?

| Setting           | Description                 | Privacy Advantage                          |
| ----------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Read Receipts     | Shows message has been read | Others won't know when you saw the message |
| Typing Indicators | Shows you are typing        | Your online activity remains hidden        |
