# Protests: Communicate Often and Safely

A successful protest requires a clear communication plan for monitoring developments, coordinating actions, and responding to emergencies. Being prepared for unexpected situations means knowing who to contact, when, and how.

## Establish an External Contact

You or a member of your affinity group should maintain regular communication with a trusted contact outside the protest who can raise the alarm if something happens.

**With your external contact, agree on:**
- Scheduled check-in times
- Security indicators (code words or signals)
- Actions to take if something goes wrong

If necessary, coordinate through civil society organisations and human rights groups.

## Prepare Emergency Contact Information

Create a list of people and organisations you can contact in emergencies, including:
- A lawyer
- Trusted friends or family
- Legal aid organizations
- Human rights groups

**Storage tips:**
- If storing on your mobile, consider using codenames
- Carry a written backup copy in case you lose your phone, leave it behind, or run out of battery
- Consider writing the most critical numbers on your arm or leg in case possessions are lost or confiscated

## Understand Surveillance Risks

Mobile phone calls, SMS messages, and many common messenger applications are easy to eavesdrop on. Authorities and other adversaries increasingly monitor these channels during protests.

**Key considerations:**
- Simply having your mobile device at a protest can identify you
- Location data and metadata can be used against you
- Unencrypted communications may be intercepted

## Use Encrypted Communication Tools

If you decide to use mobile phones, choose end-to-end encrypted applications.

### During the Protest

| Purpose | Recommended Tool | Platform |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| Encrypted messaging and calls | Signal | Android, iPhone |
| Encrypted SMS | Silence | Android |

### Before and After the Protest

| Purpose | Recommended Tool | Platform |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| Encrypted email | GPG with Thunderbird | Computer |
| Encrypted voice/video calls | Jitsi Meet | Browser, Mobile |
| Encrypted chat | Jitsi | Computer |
| Encrypted chat | ChatSecure | Mobile |
