# Privacy Tools and Service Recommendations

If you're looking for a specific solution to something, these are the hardware and software tools recommended in a variety of categories. These privacy tools are primarily chosen based on security features, with additional emphasis on decentralized and open-source tools. They are applicable to a variety of threat models ranging from protection against global mass surveillance programs and avoiding big tech companies to mitigating attacks.

## Private Web Browsers

### Tor Browser (Desktop & Android)
The top choice for anonymity, providing access to the **Tor network**—a group of volunteer-operated servers that improves privacy and security on the Internet. Individuals and organizations can share information over the Tor network with ".onion hidden services" without compromising their privacy. Tor is an effective censorship circumvention tool.

### Other Recommended Browsers

| Browser | Platform | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Mullvad Browser** | Desktop | Version of Tor Browser without Tor network integration, providing anti-fingerprinting technologies for VPN users |
| **Firefox** | Desktop/Mobile | Strong privacy settings with Enhanced Tracking Protection to block various types of tracking |
| **Brave Browser** | Desktop/Mobile | Private-by-default browser based on Chromium with minimal website compatibility issues |
| **Cromite** | Android | Chromium-based with built-in ad-blocking and privacy enhancements (fork of Bromite) |
| **Safari** | iOS | Recommended for anti-fingerprinting features and default tracker blocking |

### Browser Extensions
- **uBlock Origin** - Content blocker
- **uBlock Origin Lite** - Lightweight version
- **AdGuard for iOS** - iOS ad blocking

### Tor Network Tools
- **Onion Browser** - Tor for iOS

## VPN Providers

> **Important:** VPNs do not provide anonymity. Using a VPN will **not** keep your browsing habits anonymous, nor will it add additional security to non-secure (HTTP) traffic. For **anonymity**, use the Tor Browser. For added **security**, ensure you're connecting to websites using HTTPS.

### Top 3 Recommended VPNs
1. **Proton VPN**
2. **IVPN**
3. **Mullvad**
