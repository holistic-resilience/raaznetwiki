# About Privacy Guides

**Privacy Guides** is a socially motivated website that provides information for protecting your data security and privacy. We are a non-profit project with a mission to inform the public about the value of digital privacy and about global government initiatives which aim to monitor your online activity. Our website is free of advertisements and not affiliated with any of the listed providers.

- [Become a Member](https://donate.magicgrants.org/privacyguides)
- [Homepage](https://www.privacyguides.org/)
- [Source Code](https://github.com/privacyguides/privacyguides.org)

Privacy Guides is built by volunteers and staff members around the world. All changes to our recommendations and resources are reviewed by at least two trusted individuals, and we work diligently to ensure our content is updated as quickly as possible to adapt to the ever-changing cybersecurity threat landscape.

In addition to our core team, [many other people](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/about/contributors/) have made contributions to the project. You can too! We're open source on GitHub and accepting translation suggestions on [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/privacyguides).

## Contact Us

### Community Support

The best way to get individual help is from our community on Discourse:
- **Website Issues**: Open a discussion in the [Site Development](https://discuss.privacyguides.net/c/site-development/7) category
- **General Questions**: Ask in the [Questions](https://discuss.privacyguides.net/c/privacy/questions/8) category

### Secure Contact

For tips or sensitive information, contact us via **Signal**: `@privacyguides.01`

This group account is monitored by Jonah, Niek, Em, and Jordan.

### Email Contact

- **Team Email**: [team@privacyguides.org](mailto:team@privacyguides.org) (shared inbox)
- **Response Time**: Within 3 business days
- **OpenPGP**: Available for individual team members via WKD or [keys.openpgp.org](https://keys.openpgp.org/)

> **Note**: We are unable to provide individualized advice to everyone. For detailed responses, please use our [community forum](https://discuss.privacyguides.net/c/privacy/questions/8).
