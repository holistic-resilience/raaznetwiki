# Protests: Debrief, Evaluate and Learn

After a protest, hold a debrief meeting with your affinity group in a safe space. This session serves three essential purposes:

## Key Objectives

- **Threat Assessment**: Evaluate any ongoing risks and prevent further harm
- **Participant Care**: Support anyone experiencing distress from the action
- **Lessons Learned**: Identify improvements for future protests or actions

## Communication Guidelines

Practice patience and [active listening](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_listening) throughout the debrief. Ensure everyone understands the lessons learned and agrees on next steps.

## Addressing Injustice

If you or your group experienced unjust treatment:

1. **Seek legal advice** from your lawyer or local human rights organizations first
2. **Consider filing complaints** with relevant authorities or civil society groups
3. **Get expert guidance** before sharing any gathered information, especially if there are safety or legal implications
