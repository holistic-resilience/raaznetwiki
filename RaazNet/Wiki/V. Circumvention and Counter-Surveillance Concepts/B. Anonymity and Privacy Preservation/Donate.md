# Donate to Privacy Guides

Support Privacy Guides' mission to defend digital rights and spread awareness about mass surveillance and privacy invasions. Your contributions help researchers, activists, and maintainers create informative content, host private digital services, and protect privacy rights.

## Ways to Donate

### Membership Options

- **[Become a Member](https://donate.magicgrants.org/privacyguides/membership)** - Regular monthly or yearly support
- **[Cryptocurrency Membership](https://donate.magicgrants.org/privacyguides/membership)** - Support using Monero, Litecoin (MWEB), or Bitcoin
- **[One-Time Donation](https://donate.magicgrants.org/privacyguides/donate/privacyguides)** - Single contribution
- **[GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/privacyguides)** - Donate through GitHub

MAGIC Grants serves as Privacy Guides' fiscal host, with an open-source donation platform accepting **Monero**, **Litecoin (MWEB)**, **Bitcoin**, and **debit/credit cards**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is organizational membership?

Organizational membership is open to any company, private foundation, or organization donating at least **$5,000 per year**. Donations may be tax-deductible, and receipts are provided. Note: Organizational members cannot influence content recommendations on the website.

### What is an active membership?

Monthly or yearly memberships sustain Privacy Guides' services and public activism. Active members receive recognition on the website, community forum, and occasionally in videos (if publicly disclosed).

### How are donations used?

Donations go to a [dedicated fund](https://magicgrants.org/funds/privacy_guides) within [MAGIC Grants](https://magicgrants.org/), a 501(c)(3) organization. Funds are used exclusively for Privacy Guides:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Payroll** | Journalists, writers, and video creators who review products and create educational content |
| **Web Hosting** | Infrastructure to handle hundreds of gigabytes of monthly traffic |
| **Online Services** | Hosting [internet services](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/about/services/) for testing and community use (SearXNG, Tor, etc.) |
| **Product Purchases** | Testing [recommended tools](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/tools/) |

### Tax Deductions

Donations may qualify for tax deductions. Receipts are available when donating through the [official donation page](https://donate.magicgrants.org/privacyguides). For questions about other transactions, contact [info@magicgrants.org](mailto:info@magicgrants.org).

### Important Notes

- Donations are **not** used for political campaigns or lobbying
- Earnings do **not** benefit any private shareholder or individual
