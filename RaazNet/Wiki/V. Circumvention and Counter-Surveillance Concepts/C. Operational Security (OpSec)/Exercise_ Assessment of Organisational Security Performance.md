# Assessment of Organisational Security Performance

## Purpose & Output

This exercise helps team members collectively evaluate how well their organization implements security measures. By visualizing security performance across multiple domains, teams can identify gaps, understand different perspectives within the organization, and prioritize areas for improvement.

**Expected outcomes:**
- Visual representation of current security implementation
- Identification of barriers to better security practices
- Actionable solutions for improvement
- Consensus on priority areas

## Input & Materials

- Drawing materials (paper, colored pencils/markers)
- Or a printed copy of the [security wheel template](https://holistic-security.tacticaltech.org/ckeditor_assets/attachments/31/appendixe.pdf)

## Format & Steps

You may focus on overall organisational security performance, or one specific aspect such as:
- Digital security
- Psycho-social well-being
- Travel security
- Security in conflict zones

### Step 1: Prepare the Security Wheel

Take a copy of the [security wheel](https://holistic-security.tacticaltech.org/ckeditor_assets/attachments/31/appendixe.pdf) or draw a circle divided into eight sections, each with a relevant security domain title.

### Step 2: Self-Assessment

For each segment, color in a proportion that reflects how well your organization currently implements best practices in that area.

### Step 3: Identify Barriers

For each segment, note the barriers preventing you or the organization from better observing best practices.

### Step 4: Propose Solutions

Consider and document potential solutions for each barrier identified.

### Step 5: Compare and Discuss

Share results among team members:
- Where is there consensus?
- Where do perceptions differ?
- Why might differences exist?

### Step 6: Prioritize

Together, identify which areas must be prioritized for improvement based on the collective assessment.
