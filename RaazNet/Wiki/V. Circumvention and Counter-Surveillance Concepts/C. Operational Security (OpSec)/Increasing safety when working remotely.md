# Increasing safety when working remotely

## Introduction

The spread of the coronavirus worldwide has caused the temporary closure of many companies and led employees to turn to remote work. This experience is new and confusing for many people and can be exploited by cybercriminals.

## Key Cyber Security Tips for Remote Work

### 1. Watch out for phishing attacks

- Be suspicious of emails requesting a password change
- They might be part of phishing attacks

### 2. Caution when dealing with unknown emails

- Pay attention to emails from unknown individuals
- There is no need to open or reply to every email

### 3. Wi-Fi network security

- Ensure your Wi-Fi connection is secure

### 4. Updating software

- Keep the operating system up to date
- Update application and security software (such as antivirus)

### 5. Information encryption

- Use encryption tools
- Encrypt sensitive data when sending and receiving

## Conclusion

Observing these tips helps increase your security against attackers. By saving these items or placing them in your workspace, keep your cyber hygiene at a higher level.
