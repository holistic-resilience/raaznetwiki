## Key Takeaways

1. **Use a password manager** with a strong master passphrase
2. **Enable two-factor authentication** on all important accounts
3. **Consider passkeys** for phishing-resistant authentication
4. **Audit browser extensions** regularly and remove unnecessary ones
5. **Learn to recognize phishing** attempts before clicking links
6. **Have a breach response plan** ready before you need it
