# Security Tips for Online Shopping

With the spread of the Coronavirus and the implementation of home quarantine plans, the popularity of online shopping has increased significantly. At the same time, cyber scammers are exploiting this situation to achieve their goals by selling counterfeit goods and stealing user information. This guide provides essential tips for secure online shopping.

---

## Common Threats in Online Shopping

### Price Hikes and Fake Prices

Given the economic conditions, a slight increase in prices is expected. Influencing factors include:

- Increased demand for goods
- Increased shipping and delivery costs

**Important Note:** The price increase must be reasonable. Some websites exploit the situation and announce unrealistic prices. You can find the real price of goods with a simple search.

### Delay in Shipping Time

If the item is scarce, the waiting period may be long. **Before buying:**

- Be informed about your legal rights regarding delivery delays
- Request a refund if you do not receive the order by the scheduled time

### Counterfeit Goods

Selling counterfeit products is one of the most common internet crimes. Distinguishing between a real or fake item from a photo is almost impossible. Scammers can:

- Display the image of the original item
- Send a counterfeit version or send nothing at all

**Solution:** Be sure to read user reviews about the product and the seller.

---

## Advanced Security Threats

### Bank Information Theft (Web Skimming)

Security researchers have noticed the spread of web skimmers. Cyber attackers, by hacking online stores:

- Embed malicious codes in payment pages
- Steal users' identity and banking information

**Recommendation:** Only buy from reputable and well-known sites that you have experience buying from.

### HTTPS Protocol and Fake Websites

> **Important Warning:** The presence of HTTPS alone is not a sign of security. Fake and phishing websites can also use this protocol.

In addition to checking HTTPS, pay attention to the following:

- The exact website address
- The credibility and reputation of the store
- Reviews from other users

### Malicious Targeted Ads

Stores use cookies for targeted advertising. By clicking on ads:

- Your information is collected
- You may be redirected to fraudulent sites

**Recommendation:** Ignore targeted ads.

---

## Protecting Personal Information

### Avoid Entering Unnecessary Information

- Stores do not have the right to request unnecessary information
- Even reputable stores may suffer data leaks
- Enter only the information necessary for purchase and shipping

---

## General Preventive Measures

| Action                      | Description                                                                  |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Software Update**         | Use up-to-date apps and browsers                                             |
| **Ignoring Pop-ups**        | Constant ads and requests to install extensions are usually to deceive users |
| **Caution on Public Wi-Fi** | Avoid online payments on public networks                                     |
| **Using a VPN**             | Encrypts traffic and reduces the chance of banking information eavesdropping |

---

## Summary of Key Points

1. ✅ Compare prices
2. ✅ Read user reviews
3. ✅ Buy only from reputable sites
4. ✅ Do not enter unnecessary information
5. ✅ Use a VPN
6. ⚠️ Do not rely solely on HTTPS
