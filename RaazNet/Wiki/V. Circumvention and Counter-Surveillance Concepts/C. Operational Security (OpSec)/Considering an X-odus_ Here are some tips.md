# Considering an X-odus? Here Are Some Tips

Human rights defenders and civil society members have used Twitter since its creation. [Security in a Box acknowledged this trend by 2013](https://web.archive.org/web/20131114102313/https://securityinabox.org/en/twitter_main) with a guide on secure Twitter use. After recurrent updates, [that guide still exists](https://securityinabox.org/en/tools/x-twitter/), although the platform has since become X.

Over the years, Twitter served to distribute news quickly and engage in open debates with communities sharing similar interests. Mostly accessible without an account, it helped users follow global events and connect with individuals and organizations across the globe.

## Why People Are Leaving X

Like all centralized platforms, what Twitter allowed depended on its terms of service and ownership decisions. After Elon Musk's 2022 acquisition, policies and management changed significantly. The platform became X in July 2023.

**Key changes include:**
- Access now [mostly requires an account](https://xcancel.com/about)
- [Reduced content moderation](https://www.reuters.com/technology/twitter-exec-says-moving-fast-moderation-harmful-content-surges-2022-12-03/) for abuse and disinformation
- [Dissolution of the Trust and Safety Council](https://www.reuters.com/technology/twitter-dissolves-trust-safety-council-2022-12-13/)—the advisory group addressing hate speech, child exploitation, and other platform problems
- [Reduced reach for posts containing links](https://techround.co.uk/news/musk-limiting-external-links-x/), especially to news websites
- [Increased visibility for right-leaning content](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2024/10/29/elon-musk-x-republican-democrat-twitter-election/)

As a result, X has become a more toxic environment where harassers have the upper hand. Many notable organizations have left, including [The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/media/2024/nov/13/why-the-guardian-is-no-longer-posting-on-x), [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2023/04/12/1169269161/npr-leaves-twitter-government-funded-media-label), [EDRi](https://edri.org/our-work/why-edri-is-leaving-x-and-where-to-find-us/), and [Statewatch](https://www.statewatch.org/news/2025/january/why-statewatch-is-leaving-x/).

A [campaign to kickstart a massive exodus from X](https://www.escape-x.org/) has started, and tools exist to help you stay connected with your network elsewhere.

## What to Consider Before You Leave

Think about how you currently use X:
- Distributing updates on your activities
- Getting news and current events
- Participating in political discourse
- Joining discussions on specific topics
- Sharing videos
- Networking with like-minded people
- Brand promotion

Your primary use case will help determine the best alternative platform.

## Alternatives to X

### Bluesky

[Bluesky](https://bsky.app/) is currently the most popular X alternative.

**Pros:**
- Founded by Twitter co-creator Jack Dorsey
- Customizable user experience and moderation settings
- Familiar interface for former Twitter users

**Cons:**
- Company-owned, so policies could change with new ownership
- While designed for decentralization, currently operates as a single network (though [efforts are underway to change this](https://freeourfeeds.com/))

### Mastodon

[Mastodon](https://joinmastodon.org/about) is a federated social networking platform that respects user privacy and is based on free and open-source software.

**Getting started:**
- Choose a server based on community, code of conduct, and moderation rules
- Browse the [list of Mastodon servers on joinmastodon.org](https://joinmastodon.org/servers)
- Check where people you follow on X have migrated
- Start with [Fedi.Tips](https://fedi.tips/), an unofficial non-technical guide

### Threads and Other Meta Platforms

[Threads](https://www.threads.net/) is Meta's microblogging platform. Consider it if you focus on brand advertising and already use other Meta platforms. However, it has fewer active users than alternatives.

If using Meta platforms, check Security in a Box guides for [Instagram](https://securityinabox.org/en/tools/instagram) and [Facebook](https://securityinabox.org/en/tools/facebook).

### Social Media Management Tools

Use a tool that posts to multiple platforms simultaneously without fully leaving X. Browse [topsocialtools](https://www.topsocialtools.com/) to find solutions for your needs.

### Reddit

[Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/) works well for following and joining topic-specific conversations, often without logging in. The upvote system helps surface relevant content.

### LinkedIn

[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/) suits professional networking, job searching, and industry following.

### RSS Feed Readers

For staying current on news without social media:
- [Find RSS feeds for almost any site](https://help.socialbee.com/hc/en-us/articles/29979142036759-How-Can-I-Find-the-RSS-Feed-of-a-Website)
- [Read RSS feeds in Thunderbird](https://blog.thunderbird.net/2022/05/thunderbird-rss-feeds-guide-favorite-content-to-the-inbox/)
- Use Firefox add-ons like [Feedbro](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/feedbroreader/)

### Your Own Blog or Website

If distributing activity updates and you have capacity to manage your own site, consider a blog. You can publish an RSS feed and accept comments for audience engagement.

### Newsletter or Telegram Channel

For keeping audiences updated with full content control:
- Newsletter tools: [Substack](https://substack.com/) or [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/)
- [Telegram premium accounts](https://telegram.org/faq_premium) for monetization options

## Useful Migration Tools

These tools make your transition easier:

### Cyd
[Cyd](https://cyd.social/) helps you:
- Create a local, private backup of tweets, retweets, likes, bookmarks, and direct messages
- Delete all your tweets
- Migrate data from X to Bluesky (premium version)

### OpenPortability
[OpenPortability](https://openportability.org/en/auth/signin) migrates your data and contacts from X to Bluesky and Mastodon.

### BlueNotify
[BlueNotify](https://bluenotify.app/) provides notifications for new posts from people you follow on Bluesky—a feature Bluesky doesn't currently offer natively.

### Xcancel
[Xcancel](https://xcancel.com/about) lets you read X posts without logging into the platform.
