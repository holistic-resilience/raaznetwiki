# Financial Services for Privacy Protection

This guide covers financial services that help protect your privacy when making purchases online, including payment masking services and gift card marketplaces.

## Payment Masking Services

Payment masking services provide "virtual debit cards" that you can use with online merchants without revealing your actual banking or billing information.

### Important Limitations

These financial services are **not anonymous** and are subject to "Know Your Customer" (KYC) laws, which may require your ID or other identifying information. They are primarily useful for:

- Protection from merchant data breaches
- Reducing tracking and purchase correlation by marketing agencies
- Preventing online data theft

They are **not** designed for making completely anonymous purchases.

> **Tip:** Check if your current bank or credit card provider offers native virtual card functionality. Using your existing provider's service means trusting fewer parties with your personal information.

### Privacy.com (US)

Privacy.com's free plan allows you to:
- Create up to 12 virtual cards per month
- Set spend limits on cards
- Shut off cards instantly

Paid plans provide higher limits on the number of cards that can be created monthly.

**Privacy Consideration:** By default, Privacy.com shares merchant information with your bank. Their "private spend mode" feature hides merchant details from your bank (showing only that a purchase was made with Privacy.com), though Privacy.com itself still knows where you spend money.

- [Homepage](https://privacy.com/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://privacy.com/privacy-policy)
- [Support Documentation](https://support.privacy.com/)

### MySudo (US, Paid)

MySudo provides up to 9 virtual cards depending on your plan. Paid plans include additional privacy features such as virtual phone numbers and email addresses.

**Note:** MySudo's virtual cards are currently only available via their iOS app.

- [Homepage](https://mysudo.com/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://anonyome.com/privacy-policy)
- [Support Documentation](https://support.mysudo.com/)

### Selection Criteria for Payment Masking Services

Recommended services should meet these requirements:
- Allow creation of multiple cards that shield your personal finances from merchants
- Cards must not require accurate billing address information to be provided to merchants

## Gift Card Marketplaces

Gift card marketplaces allow you to purchase gift cards for various merchants online using cryptocurrency. Some services offer ID verification for higher limits, but basic accounts typically only require an email address.

**Typical Limits:**
- Basic accounts: $5,000-10,000 per day
- ID-verified accounts: Significantly higher limits

### Coincards

Coincards allows you to purchase gift cards for a large variety of merchants across multiple countries.

- [Homepage](https://coincards.com/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://coincards.com/privacy-policy)
- [FAQ](https://coincards.com/frequently-asked-questions)

### Selection Criteria for Gift Card Marketplaces

Recommended services should:
- Accept payment in recommended cryptocurrencies
- Have no ID requirement for basic use
