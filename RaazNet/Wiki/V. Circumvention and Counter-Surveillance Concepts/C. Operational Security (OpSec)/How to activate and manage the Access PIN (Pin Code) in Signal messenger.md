# How to activate and manage the Access PIN (Pin Code) in Signal messenger

## Introduction

Activating the access PIN (Pin Code) in Signal messenger is one of the most important measures for **physical protection** of your messages. This feature prevents unauthorized individuals from accessing messenger content in case of physical access to your device.

## Why is the access PIN important?

- Preventing unauthorized access in case of device loss or theft
- Protecting messages against individuals who have temporary access to your phone
- Additional security layer besides the device screen lock
