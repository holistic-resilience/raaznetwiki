# Reviewing and Managing Location History in Google Account

## Introduction

Google saves users' location history by default. This information includes places you have visited with devices connected to your Google account.

## Why should we manage these records?

- **Privacy Protection**: Preventing long-term storage of location data
- **Reducing Digital Footprint**: Limiting data available to Google
- **Security**: In case of unauthorized access to the account, less information is at risk

## Recommendation

It is suggested to review and clear your location history periodically (every few months).
