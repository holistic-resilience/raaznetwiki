# Enabling the Relay Calls feature in Signal messenger

## Why is Relay Calls important?

When making a voice or video call on Signal, a direct connection is usually established between you and the contact. This means **your IP address is visible to the other party**.

By enabling **Relay Calls**:

- All calls pass through Signal servers
- Your IP address remains hidden from the contact
- Your approximate geographical location is not revealed

> ⚠️ **Note:** Enabling this feature may slightly reduce call quality, but provides greater security.

## Activation Steps

1. Open the **Signal** application
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Select **Privacy**
4. Go to the **Calls** section
5. Enable **Always Relay Calls**

## Who should enable this feature?

- Civil activists and journalists
- People who call anonymous contacts
- Those concerned about revealing their geographical location
